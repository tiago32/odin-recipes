Portuguese Chicken

Home

Description

Portuguese chicken is a popular dish in Portugal. It is a spicy chicken dish that is usually grilled or roasted.

The chicken is marinated in a mixture of spices, garlic, and lemon juice before being cooked. The result is a flavorful and tender chicken that is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes, or to taste
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 chicken leg quarters
  • 1 pinch sea salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine the lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, paprika, oregano, sea salt, chili powder, red pepper flakes, bay leaf, and black pepper. Blend until smooth.
  2. Place the chicken in a large resealable plastic bag. Pour the marinade over the chicken, seal the bag, and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight.
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  4. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the marinade. Season the chicken with a pinch of sea salt.
  5. Grill the chicken on the preheated grill until no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear, about 30 to 35 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
