Caldo Verde

Description

Caldo Verde is a traditional Portuguese soup made with potatoes, collard greens, and chorizo. It is a hearty and flavorful soup that is perfect for a cold day.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 1 medium onion, minced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 6 medium potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 quarts cold water
  • 6 ounces linguiça sausage, thinly sliced
  • 2 ½ teaspoons kosher salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pound kale, rinsed and julienned

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until soft, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the potatoes and water to the pot and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and simmer until the potatoes are tender, about 20 minutes.
  3. While the potatoes are cooking, heat the remaining 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the linguiça and cook until browned, about 5 minutes.
  4. When the potatoes are tender, use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Add the kale to the pot and cook until wilted, about 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in the linguiça and serve hot.
